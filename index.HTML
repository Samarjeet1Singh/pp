WHO WE ARE

Established in 1995, and located in a Prime Industrial Estate in Leicester, England. We are one of UK’s Leading Manufacturers and Suppliers for Narrows Fabrics in terms of Elastic Braids, Shock Cords, and so much more. The Company benefits from a wealth of knowledge and a combined experience of more than fifty years in the field. Throughout this time, we have had the pleasure of working with a number of high profile brands and manufacturers. We also supply bespoke products to meet individual customer’s needs. A vast range of industry is served with our products, including various products made to British Standard and to MOD Specifications. The Company’s aim is to produce an ongoing commitment to supply quality goods and services to the industry.
